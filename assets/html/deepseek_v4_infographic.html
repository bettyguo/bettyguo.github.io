Four architectural levers, pulled together.

ATTN · SPARSE

CSA
Compressed Sparse Attn.

Compresses m=4 consecutive KV entries into one, then routes each query through a lightning indexer to the top-k most relevant blocks. DSA applied to the compressed layer.

top-k = 1024 (Pro) · 512 (Flash)
ATTN · DENSE

HCA
Heavily Compressed Attn.

Consolidates m′=128 KV entries per block, dense attention over the drastically shorter resulting sequence. Interleaved with CSA layer-by-layer.

compression ratio 128× · no sparsity
RESIDUAL

mHC
Manifold-Constrained HC.

Projects the residual mixing matrix onto the Birkhoff polytope of doubly stochastic matrices via Sinkhorn-Knopp. Bounds spectral norm ≤ 1, stabilizes deep stacks.

expansion nhc = 4 · tmax = 20
OPTIMIZER

Muon
Hybrid Newton-Schulz.

Orthogonalized momentum via two-stage Newton-Schulz (8 aggressive + 2 stabilizing). Faster convergence, better training stability. AdamW retained for embeddings & norms.

8 steps (3.44, −4.78, 2.03) → 2 steps (2, −1.5, 0.5)

Precision as an architectural lever.

THE LOSSLESS TRICK

FP4 expert weights, dequantized to FP8 — without loss.

MXFP4 (E2M1) has 2 fewer exponent bits than FP8 E4M3. The dynamic-range headroom in FP8 is exactly wide enough to absorb the per-sub-block (1×32) scale factors inside each 128×128 FP8 quantization tile, provided max/min scales stay below a bounded ratio — a condition DeepSeek empirically verifies on learned weights.

The consequence: the entire QAT pipeline reuses the existing FP8 framework unchanged. Gradients flow to FP32 masters via straight-through estimator. No re-quantization of transposed weights, no backward rewriting.

At 7.2 RMB/USD, V4-Pro output ≈ $3.33 / M tok — one-seventh of Opus 4.7, one-ninth of GPT-5.5 — for a 1.6T model, natively 1M-context. The arbitrage case for open-weight Chinese frontier models gets quantitatively harder to ignore.

What a careful reviewer should still ask.

  • Efficiency baselines are vs. V3.2 (already DSA-based), not vanilla GQA-8. Honest anchoring matters — the 2%-of-baseline figure is the one a systems paper should lead with.
  • The "3–6 month behind frontier" trajectory is self-characterized. The SimpleQA-Verified gap (57.9 vs. 75.6) implies the factuality deficit scales more steeply than the reasoning gap closes.
  • Retrieval fidelity drops with context length across all tested models at 1M (V4-Pro MRCR: 0.94 @ 32K → 0.59 @ 1024K). This is a property of the evaluation regime, not V4 specifically — "native 1M" describes architectural feasibility, and downstream use at extreme lengths needs length-aware calibration regardless of model.
  • Anticipatory Routing and SwiGLU clamping are empirically necessary, mechanistically unexplained. The authors flag this; it warrants a dedicated stability study.
  • Agentic evaluations use an internal harness (500 steps, 512K ctx). Cross-harness reproducibility with Terminal-Bench 2.0 Verified should be verified independently.
  • The Chinese-writing win-rate of 62.7% vs. Gemini is partly attributed to Gemini's "stylistic preferences overriding user requirements" — a judgment call reviewers should audit.
